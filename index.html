About My Favorite Foods

Food is one of the best things in life! It makes us happy, full, and energized. Everyone has their own list of favorite foods, and here are mine. In this page, I will share the top 5 foods that I really love to eat: Adobo, Nuggets, Mango Grahams, Tapioca, and Salad. These foods are simply delicious and I never get tired of eating them!

Adobo

Adobo is a classic Filipino dish that is truly delicious! It is made by cooking meat (usually chicken or pork) in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices. It has a savory and slightly tangy taste that goes perfectly well with hot rice.

Nuggets

Who doesn't love nuggets? They are crispy on the outside and juicy inside! Usually made from chicken, they are perfect as a snack or a main meal. They taste great especially when dipped in ketchup, barbecue sauce, or mayonnaise.

More About My Favorites

Mango Grahams

A very delicious dessert made with layers of graham crackers, cream, and sweet ripe mangoes. It's cold, creamy, and tastes like heaven!

Tapioca

A sweet and chewy snack or drink! It is made from cassava starch balls mixed with milk, sugar, and sometimes flavored with tea or fruits. Very fun to eat!

Salad

Whether it's a vegetable salad or a creamy fruit salad, it's always healthy and yummy! It is fresh, colorful, and good for the body.
